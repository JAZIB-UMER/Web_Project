Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common pricing questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time? +

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time, and the new features will be immediately available. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Any unused resume credits will carry over.

What happens if I exceed my monthly resume limit? +

If you reach your monthly limit, you can purchase additional resume credits at $0.50 per resume for the Starter plan, $0.30 for Professional, and $0.20 for Business. Alternatively, you can upgrade to a higher plan for more included resumes.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions? +

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profits, educational institutions, and government organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information and to verify your eligibility.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with access to all Professional plan features and up to 50 resume parses. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. If you cancel, you'll retain access to your plan until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. For Enterprise plans, we can also accommodate invoicing and bank transfers.
