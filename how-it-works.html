Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our process

How accurate is ResuScan's parsing? +

ResuScan achieves 99%+ accuracy across all major resume fields. Our AI system continuously learns from new data, improving its accuracy over time. For rare or unusual formats, our system will flag potential uncertainties for human review.

What file formats are supported? +

ResuScan supports all common resume formats including PDF, DOCX, DOC, RTF, TXT, and HTML. We can also parse resumes received via email attachments or from cloud storage services like Google Drive and Dropbox.

How does ResuScan handle different languages? +

Our system currently supports 30+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and Arabic. The AI automatically detects the language and applies the appropriate parsing rules for that language.

Is my data secure with ResuScan? +

Absolutely. ResuScan employs enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data centers, and strict access controls. We are GDPR compliant and never share your data with third parties. All data is processed in compliance with relevant data protection regulations.

How long does it take to process a resume? +

Individual resumes are processed in seconds. Batch uploads are processed at a rate of approximately 1,000 resumes per minute, depending on the complexity and format of the documents.

Can I customize what data is extracted? +

Yes, our Enterprise plan allows for custom field extraction. You can define specific data points relevant to your industry or organization, and our AI will be trained to identify and extract this information.
