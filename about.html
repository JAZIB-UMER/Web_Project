Our Mission

We're transforming the way companies find and hire talent by making resume analysis faster, smarter, and more objective.

Our Story

ResuScan began in 2023 when our founders, former recruiters and AI engineers, joined forces to solve the inefficiencies they experienced in the hiring process. After spending countless hours manually reviewing resumes, they realized there had to be a better way.

With backgrounds spanning machine learning, natural language processing, and talent acquisition, our team developed an AI-powered solution that could extract, analyze, and organize resume data with unmatched precision.

Today, ResuScan is trusted by over 5,000 companies worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, to streamline their recruitment processes and identify the best talent faster than ever before.
